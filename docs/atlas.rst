Introduction
============
The ATLAS pipeline is focused on the comparison of multiple strains or species. Here are the possible starting points for using the pipeline:

* An SBML model in `.xml`, `.mat`, or `.json` formats
* A list of gene IDs (`['b0001', 'b0002', ...]`)
* A dictionary of gene IDs and their sequences (`{'b0001':'MSAVEVEEAP..', 'b0002':'AERAPLS', ...}`)
